About QRCapture

QRCapture was built with a simple mission: help local businesses grow their customer lists without the friction of app downloads or complicated signup forms.

Our Story

We noticed that restaurants, bars, and lounges were missing out on valuable customer connections. Guests would visit, have a great time, and leave—never to be seen again. There was no easy way to stay in touch.

Traditional solutions required app downloads, complicated loyalty programs, or intrusive data collection. We knew there had to be a better way.

QRCapture is that better way. A simple QR code that any smartphone can scan, leading to a quick form where customers can opt-in to receive updates. No apps, no friction, just connection.

Built by Von Base Enterprises

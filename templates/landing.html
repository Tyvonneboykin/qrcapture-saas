Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after the free trial?

After your 7-day trial, you'll be charged $49/month. You can cancel anytime before the trial ends and won't be charged.

Do customers need to download an app?

No! Customers simply scan the QR code with their phone's built-in camera. It opens a mobile-friendly web page where they enter their info.

Can I customize the capture page?

Yes! You can customize the welcome message, thank you message, and brand colors to match your venue's style.

Is customer data secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell or share your customer information with third parties.
